Parmesan Potato Wedges

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 medium baking potatoes (about 8 ounces each)
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Mix first 5 ingredients.
  2. Cut each potato lengthwise into 8 wedges; place in a parchment-lined 15x10x1-in. pan. Spritz with cooking spray; sprinkle with cheese mixture. Bake until tender, about 30 minutes.
